About Me

Coming back to the school I love and support two years after my original graduation is an honor and I wouldn't have it any other way! My first love in life was definitely sports, so naturally I spend a lot of my free time either watching or playing them. After a long day of coding, there is nothing better than heading to the gym and getting the blood flowing!
